FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to know how to code or design? +
Not at all. GetBookKing handles everything — design, hosting, and setup. You just fill in your info and your site is ready to go.
Can I use my own domain name? +
Yes — custom domains are available on the Shop plan. Solo and Studio plans include a free yourname.getbookking.com subdomain.
What happens after the free month? +
You'll be prompted to choose a plan before your trial ends. If you don't upgrade, your site is paused — your data is never deleted.
Can I switch plans later? +
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
How does booking work for my clients? +
Clients visit your site, pick a service and time slot, and book directly. You get notified instantly, and they get an automatic confirmation and reminder.
Can I add my own photos and branding? +
Absolutely. You can upload your own photos, set your brand colors, and customize every section of your site from the settings editor.
